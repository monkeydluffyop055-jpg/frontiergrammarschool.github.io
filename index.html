About Us

Our Story, Mission & Vision

Our Story

About Frontier Grammar School

Frontier Grammar School (FGS) was established with a clear mission: to provide high-quality, English-medium education to the children of Bachken Ahmad Zai, Lakki Marwat — free of financial barriers. Located in the heart of the community, FGS serves students from Play Group through Class 8th.

Our institution is led by a passionate principal and managed by a dedicated director who believe deeply in the transformative power of education. With a team of 11 qualified teachers — both male and female — FGS delivers a curriculum that blends academic excellence with strong moral values.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, quality English-medium education from early childhood to Class 8th, nurturing intellectual curiosity, moral integrity, and lifelong love of learning in every student.

Our Vision

To become the leading primary school in Lakki Marwat — recognized for academic excellence, character development, and equal opportunity for every child regardless of background.

Our Values

Excellence · Integrity · Inclusion · Islamic Ethics · Community Service · Innovation in Education.
